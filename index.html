Dog

Dogs, Canis lupus familiaris, are domesticated mammals known for their loyalty and companionship with humans. They come in various breeds, sizes, and temperaments, serving roles ranging from family pets to working animals, such as service dogs, herders, and search-and-rescue partners.

Cat

Cats, scientifically known as Felis catus, are small, carnivorous mammals often kept as pets for companionship. They exhibit a wide range of breeds, sizes, and coat patterns, and are known for their agility, grooming behavior, and independent nature.

Horse

Horses are large, domesticated mammals known for their strength, speed, and versatility. They have played a crucial role in human history, serving as transportation, companions, and work animals. Horses come in various breeds, each with distinct characteristics, and are often admired for their grace and beauty.

Rabbit

Rabbits are small mammals known for their long ears, short tails, and agile hind legs. They are herbivores and are popular as pets due to their gentle nature. Rabbits are prolific breeders and are found in a variety of habitats worldwide.

Sheep

Sheep are domesticated mammals known for their woolly coats, which are commonly used for textiles. They are herbivores, primarily grazing on grass, and are often raised for their wool, meat, and milk. Sheep are social animals that tend to flock together and have been bred for various purposes by humans for thousands of years.

Pigs

Pigs, scientifically known as Sus scrofa domesticus, are domesticated mammals commonly raised for meat production. They are highly intelligent animals with a keen sense of smell, and they are known for their social nature. Pigs are omnivores and are often raised on farms for pork consumption worldwide.
